Hello ! ... Ram Ram ... ! this is sarpanch of Rajiv Nagar Jhiri and Gram Panchyat . We welcome you to official website of Rajiv Nagar Jhiri .Rajiv Nagar is a village from Hisar district of Haryana. As time is changing we are also trying to be computerized from the tedious and time - consuming paperwork. They can do complains online . Now they don't have to walk long 2 km just for filing a complaint . But we are also going to add a picture gallery of our village. Also also share glorious history of our village . This will help in attaracting tourists and people from outside will be more respectful to our village .
